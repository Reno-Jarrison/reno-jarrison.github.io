2021-05-19
English Writing

Sports Bring Me More Than the Health

 Sports are definitely beneficial for body building and physical health, but I get a lot more than these from sports. I started keeping doing some sports in my daily life a month ago. The sports I keep doing include outdoor running and indoor exercises, such as push-ups, squats, sit-ups and so on. And in one week, I usually do exercise in five days, three for running and two for indoor exercises alternatively. Then, I’m goint to point out what I have got from one-month sports.

 First of all, I do feel better with my body and health. The most obvious change of my body is that I lose weight. It is more important that I improve my daily routine by exercising. Before my exercise plan started, my life routine is a mess. That is, I always stayed up late and got up late. Also I kept skipping the breakfast. However, in this month, because of adequate exercise in the evening, I gain better sleep so that I can get up early and have breakfast. And thanks to this, I feel more energetic in everyday work and study.

 Then, sports make me feel a sense of controlling myself. When doing exercises, especially unarmed exercises like push-up, I am able to feel my body’s movement and effort so that I can better control my strength. Besides, I can see my growth in this month. The running distance gets longer while my muscle power also gets stronger. On the other side, I also feel that I can master my daily life. Under the influence of regular sports, my life becomes regular as well. And gradually I am able to see what I gain in one day.

 Sports also relieve me from low spirits. Actually, sometimes I get nervous or have a down mood. In this situation, I will try to do some sports without keeping anything in mind. Totally, it works well and always get me out of my emotions before the end of the day. And it refreshes me to have a better start of tomorrow.

 Finally, sports contributes building my confidence. In the beginning time of my sports plan, it is always hard to insist to take exercise. But every time I accomplish my expected running distance or exercise amounts, I feel a great sense of achievement. At the same time, it provides me a belief that I can do what I want to do. And I think this is the most important benefit I gain from sports.

 Gernerally speaking, during one-month sports, I gain a lot both physically and mentally. And also, I will carry on.

English Writing

Ignite the Spark in Life

​  Recently, I watched a meaningful and impressive movie called $Soul$ by Pixar. It tells about how a music teacher finds his values of life. This movie encouraged me a lot, and I also got a good deal of enlightenment from it.

 The main story of $Soul$ is that Joey, a talented pianolist but also a middle school music teacher, is provided an opportunity to play jazz with some of the best musicians in a club. However, on the way to the club, by accident, he falls into a well and gets his own soul out of body. Then, he comes to $The\ Great\ Before$，where the souls of baby stays before going to the earth. And Joey meets a baby soul here and wants to help it find its spark to complete the passport to the earth so that he can turn back with it. But the baby soul can not find any spark like arts, science, cooking and so on in $The\ Great\ Before$ until they come to the earth. This time, the baby soul enters into the body of Joey while Joey becomes a cat. As the first time to live, the baby soul experiences a lot of wonderful moments in life and finds its spark already ignited. After that, Joey as the successful mentor of the baby soul is given a chance by the world manager to come to life. And when he finally accomplishs his performance and realizes his lifelong dream, he feels upset and lost instead. But later, he realizes that the spark of himself is just playing jazz and really finds the meaning of life.

 I think we indeed need to stop our steps sometime to feel the life in every second. The most impressive word in this movie is that “When you are ready to live, the spark of your life is already ignited”. The major of us regard the achievement of something as the ultimate goal of our life. But at the same time, we ignore the every little things of daily life. Just like the jazz, a casual and improvised music form, our life is full of various possibilities and will keep going whatever happens. So it is pretty hard to demand our life to develop exactly as what we think.

 Actually, I also feel a little anxious about my schoolwork and the future these days. I’m somewhat lost in the decision of future direction. But this movie does inspire me to focus more on the present and to feel the beauty of life. When I realize there still exist many loved and beloved people of mine, and plenty of things deserved to do, it really makes me love my life further.

English Writing

Childhood and Farewell

 This evening I finished reading $My\ Memories\ of\ Old\ Beijing$ by Lin Haiyin. This book is talking about the childhood of Yinzi, the heroine, and shows the partings and reunions of people from the perspective of a child.

 Reading this book, I recalled those carefree days in my childhood. In that time, I didn’t need to worry about my school work or my future. Instead, I could find enjoyment from little things in my normal life. For example, I could chase the butterflies or just watch the river flowing for hours. Similarly, in Yinzi’s view, things around the world are wonderful and pure, which form a sharp contrast to the reality in adults’ eyes.

  Except for the wonderful childhood this book describes, the sad feelings of departure touch me more. As she grows up, Yinzi gradually witnesses the departure of the relatives and friends around her. With a realistic perspective, most of the stories told in this book are tragedies. However, with Yinzi’s perspective, the innocence of children is reflected. Eventually, the book ends with the passing away of Yinzi’s father, which she realizes is a farewell to her childhood. From then on, as the eldest offspring in her family, she has to take responsibility for the whole family.

 Actually, when I was young, my grandfather passed away. My grandfather was an intellectual and he always told stories and wrote verses to me. Yet until I grew up, I realized what is the meaning that a loved person leaves forever. Everytime I remembered the happy time I stayed with him, I would miss him very much.

 In gerneral, my life in the childhood was full of happiness. I think growing up is a continual process of farewells to people and things around, including the childhood. Although my actual childhood has already gone, the soul of it was kept deep in my mind.

Farewell to Programming Competition

 Today, with the end of ICPC Asia East Continent Final, my career on programming contest is coming to an end. The ICPC, International Collegiate Programming Contest, is a famous competitive contest in the area of computer science, during which the contestants are grouped in three and given five hours to solve a dozen of difficult problems by one computer. Unfortunately, in this contest, my team missed the award. Although the result of this contest is not satisfactory enough, I have a lot of feelings inside.

 Actually, in the mid-term of the contest, I have a sense that we are going to loss the award. The direct reason is that we took a wrong strategy to choose problems. We underestimated the difficulty of one problem and keep struggling against it while ignoring the easier one. But frankly speaking, we are indeed lack of training before the contest.

 However, three of us are not too disappointed with this defeat or something. And we are more likely to enjoy the process of solving problems. With deep thoughts, heated discussion and exciting submitting, each ‘accepted’ we get provides us powerful motivation to carry on. That really inspires people. Also, because all three of us know that we have already achieved plenty of good grades before, and that today is our last time to participate the programming contest on the scene, we have no burden instead and just want to feel the tense atmosphere of competition.

 From my perspective, programming contest gives me more than the awards. The awards are definitely important, which indicates my level and encourages me to move on. Besides, this contest stimulate my interest in computer and algorithms greatly. Moreover, to perform well in the contest, a lot of superclass knowledge and continual practice are necessitated, that is challengeable and needs persistence. Additionally, thanks to the programming contest, I gained wonderful friendship and met with remarkable people.

 Generally, two year competition career is one of the most meaningful experiences in my college life. I appreciate the days and nights I devoted to coding and I really enjoy it. Farewell!
